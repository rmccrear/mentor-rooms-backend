Instructions

When you wish to start a tutoring session, click Clock In and share the STUDENT LINK with students.

If there are too many students, you can click ROOM FULL to stop them from entering. (Be sure to click it again to open the room again)

Remember to CLOCK OUT when done.

Note: Clock In Times and Clock Out times are not recorded. If you need that information, keep track of it yourself.

This can be shared with students when they have breakout rooms or labs and they can pop in to your room to ask a question.

Rules for students

  • Please ask your questions upon entring the room.
  • The mentor will finish working with those ahead of you in line before answering your question.
  • The mentor may work with more than one person at the same time.
  • Each person can have 20 minutes for their question. After the question has been answered, please leave the room so another can join.
